History of City University of Pasay


City Ordinance 271 series of 1994, established the Pamantasan ng Lungsod ng Pasay as a tertiary or
higher institution of learning for and in the City of Pasay. To be exact, it was enacted on May 26, 1994.

PLP started with 384 students and 11 faculty members, holding evening class at the Pasay City West High School.
The Division of City School Pasay City played a significant role in the development of the Pamantasan
providing not only consultative services but also personnel services and facilities.
